# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Developers starting and connecting local Docker Compose and host-native
applications. They use Localghost from a terminal while focused on their own
application, and need concise feedback they can trust at a glance.

## Product Purpose

Localghost provides easy, loopback-only `.localhost` URLs for local
applications. Its terminal feedback should make setup, status, and next steps
clear without getting in the way of normal development work.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, friendly, and reassuring. The `localghost` wordmark carries a cute,
recognisable identity, while the surrounding copy remains direct and useful.

## Anti-references

Avoid noisy mascot chatter, gaudy terminal rainbows, generic infrastructure
jargon, and decorative output that obscures the action or fix.

## Design Principles

- Introduce the tool before asking it to make a consequential choice.
- Make the next useful action obvious.
- Let the wordmark and a restrained lime accent carry personality.
- Keep routine feedback compact and script-friendly.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Do not rely on colour alone for meaning. Preserve a plain-text fallback for
non-interactive terminals and automation, with concise copy that works well in
screen readers and narrow terminal windows.
